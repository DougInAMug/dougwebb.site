Introducing Kanthaus

Two shabby buildings in a German town you’ve probably never heard of before have become home for a group of post-waste activists: we call it Kanthaus.

Located within the mesh of global challenges is the fact that 1/3 of edible food isn’t eaten. The issue of waste is a focal point for our project, inherited from our involvement with yunity.org and foodsharing.de. We believe that most waste is due to outdated economics and that sharing provides solutions. Kanthaus is a platform for people to promote sharing and/or reduce waste.
