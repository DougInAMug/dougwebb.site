Evaluation facilitation sheet

Preparation

  • Avoid distractions:
    • does anyone need the toilet?
    • are phones on silent or off?
  • Use the door indicator when ready to start.
    • it’s OK to ask late-comers not to join

Open part

Intro

“<Evaluee> has been at Kanthaus for over—

  • 3 weeks, with the position of Visitor.” or,
  • 2 months, with the position of Volunteer.” or,
  • 6 months, with the position of Member.”

“We are gathered here to reflect on the relationship between Kanthaus and <Evaluee> and consider how it continues; we do this in good faith, with the intention of helping everyone achieve their greatest potential.”

Silence

“Before we begin, I invite everyone to share some quiet moments of personal reflection.” (Count at least 10 deep, calm breaths.)

Questions to <Evaluee>

  1. “How are you feeling just now?”
  2. “What did you do during the last period?”
  3. “How do feel about the last period?”
  4. “Do you know which position you would like to go forward with? If yes, which and why?”
  5. “Is there anything else you’d like to say just now?”

Questions to everyone

  1. “Are there any questions to or from <Evaluee>?”
  2. “Are there any concerns to or from <Evaluee>?”
  3. “Is there anything else to be said before a round of appreciation?”

Round of appreciation

“Now we have a round for people to express appreciation. It’s fine to say nothing and pass on. Would <person left of Evaluee> like to start?” (Round takes place.)

“Thank you everyone. I now ask <Evaluee> and any Visitors (and Volunteers in the case <Evaluee> wants to become a Member) to leave the room.”

Closed part

Mock vote

“Do you support, accept or oppose <Evaluee> becoming/remaining a <Position>?”

Discussion

Start round left to the dealer.

Final vote

“Do you support, accept or oppose <Evaluee> becoming/remaining a <Position>?”

Finishing tasks

  • Who will give feedback?
  • Who will update the record?
  • Who will tidy the room?
